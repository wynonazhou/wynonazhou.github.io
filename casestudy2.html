FSU Virtual ID: Research Study

Humans are bound to forget things. From car keys to class notes, it is very unlikely for someone to never not loose one of their personal items. As college students, we always need our student ids for our daily campus needs. If students were to lose their ids, they wouldnâ€™t be able to do some of their daily convivences such as accessing facilities, like the library and the gym, buy food, and do other school activities. Every student nowadays seems to have a mobile device. If a student could use their phone in replacement for their id card, it could save a lot of pain. There are many locations in the world that have already implemented digital identifications, the goal is that FSU needs to believe that we actually need this. Before anything can happen though, it is fundemental that the source is analyzed to it's full extent. This being the FSU student body.


The proposed solution

Our solution was to run a semester long study on a variety of FSU students in different majors on their interests of having a student id app on their mobile devices. Near the end of the semester, we were able to come up with the solution that students do in fact have interest when it comes to a mobile student id. We were sadly unable to complete a prototype of the app in the class. However, there are several faculties on campus that are currently working on a similar type of tool, so this study could be used to assist them in this integration.
