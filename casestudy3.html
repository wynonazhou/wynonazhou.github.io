Grace Mission Episcopal Church

A website is as important as a billboard, and if you take consideration on keeping your internet presence good in order to fully grow out whatever you are trying to send to audiences about your organization. This especially goes to organizations that do not make money on other services, and need to make a appealing and easy-to-navigate site in order to influence individuals to donate to their cause.
For this project, some of my fellow classmates and I collaborated with a local church known as Grace Mission Episcopal Church. This church is known to take various individuals, especially those in need. Some examples of the church members include healing drug addicts and the homeless. There current website is quite outdated, so throughout a semester we worked together to create a portfolio that exhibited the improvements needed to the site, with the goal in mind to get approval to make the changes come into reality.


The proposed solution

In the end, I was able to create a presentation that would be useful in the rebranding for the Grace Mission Episcopal Church website. My team and I were also able to create a portfolio that was used to propose our final designs to the local church. In the future, it is planned to continue working with the church to get the changes fully implemented. This current page will show our updates as changes are made with the actual website design and structure.
