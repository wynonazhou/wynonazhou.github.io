Karmelitas Green Cleaning

The problem

To ensure that my skillset is not restricted, I always try to set outside of the box of Information Technology-focused experiences. For this specific project, I was able to experience the discipline of marketing. Specifically, I was able to gain experience with Hispanic-focused marketing through a semester-long course. For this project, I worked alongside two other teammates to provide a California-based cleaning service to rebrand their online presence. Our client, Karmelitas Green Cleaning, is a household cleaning service. Currently only servicing home properties, the owner would like to expand their customer base to hotels and offices. Due to their limited experience with their online presence, they are unsure of what they need to do to attract their customer goal. Not being able to spend much money on marketing, they have contacted our course to gain assistance on their marketing needs.


The proposed solution

By the end of the project, we produced a portfolio containing deliverables such as a prototype design of the new company website and new graphics for the social media pages. We also deliverables other items such as the client's current customer base, overall costs for all integrations, and other deliverables that pertained to the marketing project.
Due to the language barrier with the client, I was not able to present the deliverables directly. However, one of my other teammates carried on to intergrate the marketing items we compiled throughout the course.
