Karmelitas Green Cleaning

Marketing, even if it seems unrelated to Information Technology, is a fundamental topic for anyone to learn in all types of careers. Without marketing artists wouldn’t know how to brand themselves and without marketing our favorite website wouldn’t be the same as today. This was my first ever experience working with a real business, as well as my first time working on a marketing project. Because of my focus in IT, I couldn’t get into the world of marketing. As stated in the title, I had worked with a team to work with a Hispanic based marketing business. Below is a short description of the client, Karmelitas Green Cleaning.

Karmelitas Green Cleaning
Karmelitas Green Cleaning is a household cleaning service in California. The main premise behind the business is that their products has a focus on environmentally friendly supplies. With a focus on cleaning households, the owner would like to expand their customer base to hotels and offices. They don’t have very much money to spend on their marketing, but they need strengthening it, so they can expand to more customers. They hope that the marketing plan will save them the money in their past marketing attempts, with the main goal of bringing a new image to the organization.

Our role was to provide them advice and tactics in order to heighten their marketing tactics. In the next section, there will be more explanation on what elements we created for Karmelitas Green Cleaning.


The proposed solution

By the end of the project, we were able to create a portfolio containing material such as a prototype design of the new company website, new graphics for the social medias, and several pages of analysis on the customer base, overall costs and other factors that were considered while creating the items during the project.
