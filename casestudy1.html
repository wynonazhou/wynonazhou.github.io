Scrum Organization Board - The Children's Campaign

Communication is something that is stated as important, but its impact isn’t really known until the worst comes from the often times forgotten foe, bad communication. This solution was made throughout my time at the Children’s Campaign using Scrum methodology. It is very common for groups, from school to work, to have issues that arise from one factor. As stated before, there was a lack of communication in the organization that affected the morale, as well as progress of technological advancements in the organization.


The proposed solution

The proposed solution was to follow a program that some of my fellow co-workers have been using. This program was Asana, which is an online organizational tool for groups. In order to increase communication, our solution was to create boards that could be viewed by anyone in the office. It turned out to be a great success and other teams integrated this solution as well(which can be seen in the image below).
